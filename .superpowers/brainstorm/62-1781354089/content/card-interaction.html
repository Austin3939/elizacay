Shop Card — Click Behaviour

How do users get from the shop grid to the product detail page?

Click image or title → product page

A — Image & title are the link

Clicking the image or product name navigates to the detail page. "Add to Cart" still works directly from the grid. Keeps the card compact — no extra button.

IMAGE
VIEW DETAILS

One button that goes to product page

B — "View Details" replaces "Add to Cart"

The card button navigates to the product page instead. All purchasing happens on the detail page. Simpler card, but adds a step before buying.

A subtle "View →" badge appears on image hover. "Add to Cart" stays on the card. Both paths available, but hover-only doesn't work on touch devices.
